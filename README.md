# Hungerbane

Hungerbane is a quality-of-life survival tweak that changes the fundamental mechanics of hunger and healing in Minecraft. No more dying to starvation, and no more hoarding golden apples just to heal!

## Key Features

- You will no longer take damage or die from an empty hunger bar. 
- Eating food now restores your health based on its nutrition value.
- Replaces the often-useless vanilla Saturation effect. While active, it significantly boosts the healing you get from food.
- The vanilla Hunger effect now reduces healing from food
- Blinking heart overlay appears when you hold food, showing exactly how much health you will restore before you even take a bite.
- Wake up feeling refreshed! Configurable health restoration upon naturally waking up from a bed.
- Tweak every aspect of the mod on the fly without restarting your game!

## Configuration

Hungerbane comes with a user-friendly **in-game configuration screen**. Simply go to your Mod List, find Hungerbane, and click the **"Config"** button. 

You can easily adjust:
- Base healing multiplier from food.
- Healing bonus from the "Nourished" effect.
- Healing penalty from the "Hunger" effect.
- Amount of health restored after sleeping.
